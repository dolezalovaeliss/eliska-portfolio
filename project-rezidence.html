ABOUT

I designed a website for the sale of residential apartments, working closely with the art director. The core challenge was getting both the visual style and information architecture exactly right, meeting the developer’s brand requirements while staying attractive and clear for end clients.

The result reads as confident and editorial: clean typography, generous imagery, and a structure that lets prospective buyers find what they need without effort.
