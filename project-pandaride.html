ABOUT

Comprehensive app design for Panda Ride, a child transportation service. The project covered two distinct user interfaces: a parent-facing client app for booking and tracking, and a driver app for trip execution.

The focus throughout was an intuitive, quick, and maximally clear interface, designed to be used during stressful daily routines where every second of friction matters.
