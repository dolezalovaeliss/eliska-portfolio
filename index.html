About

I’m Eliška, a designer based in Prague. I started in graphic design, doing brands and websites, and spent a lot of those years around culture — working with festivals like KVIFF and Prague Pride.

Later I moved into UX, studied it during my master’s, and ended up in product design. I like it because I get to understand the whole problem, not just one screen, and help decide where a product should go — based on its vision and what’s actually happening in the market.
