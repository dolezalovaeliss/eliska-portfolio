Nice to meet you. I'm Eliska.

I'm Eliška, a product designer based in Prague. Six years of design across festivals, brand identity, B2B websites, and product UX/UI. Across many agencies I sharpened my visual craft; at Ataccama I learned to work as an independent product designer — validating solutions with the product manager, consulting with the design team, and handing off to engineers.

I work embedded, not project by project. Most engagements look like a fractional design lead — on Slack, in standups, part of the team. AI speeds up production; product-design rigor keeps the work shipping.
