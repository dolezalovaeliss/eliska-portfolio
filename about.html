Nice to meet you. I'm Eliska.

I'm Eliska, a product designer based in Prague. Six years of design across festivals, brand identity, B2B websites, and product UI, coming into product design through graphic and web work for Prague Pride, before training at Ataccama.

I work embedded, not project by project. Most engagements look like a fractional design lead, on Slack, in standups, part of the team. AI-augmented prototyping keeps production fast; product-design rigor keeps the work shipping.
